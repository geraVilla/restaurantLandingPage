Our Origin

Michoacan:

The Enchanting

Soul of Mexico

Michoacán, the 'Soul of Mexico,' boasts diverse indigenous heritage reflected in its hearty regional cuisine. Quiroga shines as the 'carnitas capital,' while Lake Pátzcuaro, the 'place of fishermen,' graces this avocado-rich land.
